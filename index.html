#FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

A static website is a site that is delivered to a user's web browser exactly as it's stored. Unlike dynamic websites, they don't require server-side processing or database calls. This makes them incredibly fast, secure, and reliable; perfect for small businesses.

Our pricing plans are all-inclusive. They cover the design and development of your website, mobile-responsive design, a contact form, hosting, and depending on the plan, features like multiple pages, and SEO optimization. There are no hidden fees.

Typically, a basic website can be launched within 1-2 weeks once we have all the necessary content from you. More complex sites might take longer. We'll give you a precise timeline after our initial consultation.

To start, we'll need your business information, any branding materials you have (like logos and color schemes), and the content for your website (text and images). If you don't have content ready, we can help you create it.

A mail-to form is a type of web form that, instead of sending the information to a website server, opens your email software (like Gmail, Outlook, or iCloud Mail) and puts the form’s information into a new email for you to send. Think of it like an email template your customers can use. You can use our contact button below to see one in action.

If you need features that require a server like database storage, email auto-responders, or payment processing, you’ll need an API integration with a third-party service. Which will incur an additional cost on top of your annual subscription. This cost depends on the additional man hours we will spend per week engineering and maintaining the integration. We will discuss the exact cost and more with you during our consultation.
